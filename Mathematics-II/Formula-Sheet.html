UNIT 1 · Determinants & Matrices

🔷 Determinants

Order 2
\(\begin{vmatrix}a&b\\c&d\end{vmatrix}=ad-bc\)
Order 3 (expansion)
\(\begin{vmatrix}a_1&b_1&c_1\\a_2&b_2&c_2\\a_3&b_3&c_3\end{vmatrix}=a_1(b_2c_3-b_3c_2)-b_1(a_2c_3-a_3c_2)+c_1(a_2b_3-a_3b_2)\)
Minor & Cofactor
\(M_{ij}\) = det after removing row i, col j
\(C_{ij}=(-1)^{i+j}M_{ij}\)
Chio's Method (4×4)
\(\Delta = a_{11}^{3}\begin{vmatrix}b_{22}&b_{23}&b_{24}\\b_{32}&b_{33}&b_{34}\\b_{42}&b_{43}&b_{44}\end{vmatrix}\) with \(b_{ij}=\frac{a_{11}a_{ij}-a_{i1}a_{1j}}{a_{11}}\)
Cramer's Rule (3 unknowns)
\(x=\frac{D_x}{D},\; y=\frac{D_y}{D},\; z=\frac{D_z}{D}\) where D = det of coefficient matrix

🔷 Matrices

Addition / Subtraction
\((A\pm B)_{ij}=a_{ij}\pm b_{ij}\)
Scalar Multiplication
\((kA)_{ij}=k\cdot a_{ij}\)
Matrix Multiplication
\((AB)_{ij}=\sum_{k=1}^{n}a_{ik}b_{kj}\)
Transpose
\((A^T)_{ij}=a_{ji}\)
Symmetric / Skew-symmetric
\(A^T=A\) (symmetric) , \(A^T=-A\) (skew-sym)
Adjoint & Inverse (3×3)
\(\text{adj}(A)=[C_{ij}]^T\)
\(A^{-1}=\frac{\text{adj}(A)}{\det(A)}\)
UNIT 2 · Co‑ordinate Geometry (2D)

📍 Coordinate Systems & Basic Formulas

Cartesian ↔ Polar
\(x=r\cos\theta,\; y=r\sin\theta\)
\(r=\sqrt{x^2+y^2},\; \theta=\tan^{-1}(y/x)\)
Distance between two points
\(d=\sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2+(y_2-y_1)^2}\)
Internal division (m:n)
\(\left(\frac{mx_2+nx_1}{m+n},\frac{my_2+ny_1}{m+n}\right)\)
External division (m:n)
\(\left(\frac{mx_2-nx_1}{m-n},\frac{my_2-ny_1}{m-n}\right)\)
Area of triangle
\(\Delta=\frac12\left|x_1(y_2-y_3)+x_2(y_3-y_1)+x_3(y_1-y_2)\right|\)

📈 Straight Line

Slope (gradient)
\(m=\frac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1}=\tan\theta\)
Slope-intercept
\(y=mx+c\)
Point-slope
\(y-y_1=m(x-x_1)\)
Two-point form
\(\frac{y-y_1}{y_2-y_1}=\frac{x-x_1}{x_2-x_1}\)
Intercept form
\(\frac{x}{a}+\frac{y}{b}=1\)
Angle between two lines
\(\tan\phi=\left|\frac{m_2-m_1}{1+m_1m_2}\right|\)
Parallel / Perpendicular
\(m_1=m_2\) (parallel)
\(m_1m_2=-1\) (perpendicular)
Distance point to line
\(d=\frac{|ax_1+by_1+c|}{\sqrt{a^2+b^2}}\)
Distance between || lines
\(d=\frac{|c_1-c_2|}{\sqrt{a^2+b^2}}\)

⚪ Circle

Centre-radius form
\((x-h)^2+(y-k)^2=r^2\)
Diameter form
\((x-x_1)(x-x_2)+(y-y_1)(y-y_2)=0\)
General equation
\(x^2+y^2+2gx+2fy+c=0\)
Centre \((-g,-f)\), radius \(\sqrt{g^2+f^2-c}\)

📦 Conic Sections

Parabola (standard)
\(y^2=4ax\) (opens right)
Focus \((a,0)\), directrix \(x=-a\), latus rectum \(4a\)
Ellipse (standard)
\(\frac{x^2}{a^2}+\frac{y^2}{b^2}=1\;(a>b)\)
Eccentricity \(e=\sqrt{1-\frac{b^2}{a^2}}\)
Foci \((\pm ae,0)\)
UNIT 3 · Integral Calculus

∫ Indefinite Integrals (Standard Forms)

\(\int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}+C\;(n\neq-1)\) \(\int \frac{1}{x}dx = \ln|x|+C\) \(\int e^x dx = e^x+C\) \(\int a^x dx = \frac{a^x}{\ln a}+C\) \(\int \sin x dx = -\cos x+C\) \(\int \cos x dx = \sin x+C\) \(\int \sec^2 x dx = \tan x+C\) \(\int \csc^2 x dx = -\cot x+C\) \(\int \sec x\tan x dx = \sec x+C\) \(\int \csc x\cot x dx = -\csc x+C\) \(\int \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}dx = \sin^{-1}x+C\) \(\int \frac{1}{1+x^2}dx = \tan^{-1}x+C\)

🔄 Methods of Integration

Substitution
\(\int f(g(x))g'(x)dx = \int f(u)du\)
Integration by parts
\(\int u\,dv = uv - \int v\,du\)
Partial fractions
\(\frac{P(x)}{(x-a)(x-b)} = \frac{A}{x-a}+\frac{B}{x-b}\)

📏 Definite Integrals & Applications

Fundamental theorem
\(\int_a^b f(x)dx = F(b)-F(a)\) where \(F'=f\)
Area between curves
\(A=\int_a^b [f(x)-g(x)]dx\)
Volume (x‑axis revolution)
\(V=\pi\int_a^b [f(x)]^2 dx\)
Volume (y‑axis)
\(V=\pi\int_c^d [g(y)]^2 dy\)
Surface area (x‑axis)
\(S=2\pi\int_a^b f(x)\sqrt{1+[f'(x)]^2}dx\)
Surface area (y‑axis)
\(S=2\pi\int_c^d g(y)\sqrt{1+[g'(y)]^2}dy\)
UNIT 4 · Ordinary Differential Equations

📌 First Order & First Degree

Separation of variables
\(\frac{dy}{dx}=f(x)g(y) \Rightarrow \int\frac{dy}{g(y)}=\int f(x)dx\)
Homogeneous
\(\frac{dy}{dx}=f(\frac{y}{x})\); substitute \(y=vx\)
Exact equation
\(Mdx+Ndy=0\) with \(\frac{\partial M}{\partial y}=\frac{\partial N}{\partial x}\)
Solution: \(\int M dx + \int (N-\frac{\partial}{\partial y}\int M dx)dy = C\)
Linear (integrating factor)
\(\frac{dy}{dx}+P(x)y=Q(x)\)
\(IF=e^{\int P dx}\)
\(y(IF)=\int Q(IF)dx + C\)

⚙️ Second Order Linear (constant coefficients)

Auxiliary equation
\(am^2+bm+c=0\)
Roots \(m_1,m_2\)
CF (real distinct roots)
\(C_1 e^{m_1x}+C_2 e^{m_2x}\)
CF (real equal roots)
\((C_1+C_2x)e^{mx}\)
CF (complex \(\alpha\pm i\beta\))
\(e^{\alpha x}(C_1\cos\beta x+C_2\sin\beta x)\)
PI for \(e^{ax}\)
\(\frac{e^{ax}}{F(a)}\) provided \(F(a)\neq0\)
PI for \(\sin ax\) or \(\cos ax\)
\(\frac{\sin ax}{F(-a^2)}\) or \(\frac{\cos ax}{F(-a^2)}\) if \(F(-a^2)\neq0\)
PI for \(e^{ax}V(x)\) (V polynomial)
\(\frac{1}{F(D)}e^{ax}V = e^{ax}\frac{1}{F(D+a)}V\)
UNIT 5 · Partial Differentiation

∂ Partial Derivatives

Definition
\(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}(a,b)=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{f(a+h,b)-f(a,b)}{h}\)
\(\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}(a,b)=\lim_{k\to0}\frac{f(a,b+k)-f(a,b)}{k}\)
Notation
\(f_x,\; f_y,\; \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2},\; \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2},\; \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x\partial y}\)

🧩 Homogeneous Functions & Euler

Homogeneous function
\(f(tx,ty)=t^n f(x,y)\) ⇒ degree n
Euler's theorem (1st order)
\(x\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}+y\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}=n f(x,y)\)
UNIT 6 · Statistics & Probability

📊 Measures of Central Tendency

Mean (ungrouped)
\(\bar{x}=\frac{\sum x_i}{n}\)
Mean (grouped)
\(\bar{x}=\frac{\sum f_i x_i}{\sum f_i}\)
Median (ungrouped)
middle value; for even \(n\) average of \(\frac{n}{2}\)th and \(\frac{n}{2}+1\)th
Median (grouped)
\(M = L + \frac{\frac{N}{2}-cf}{f}\times h\)
Mode (grouped)
\(Z = L + \frac{f_1-f_0}{2f_1-f_0-f_2}\times h\)

📉 Measures of Dispersion

Standard deviation (ungrouped)
\(\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum (x_i-\bar{x})^2}{n}}\)
Standard deviation (grouped)
\(\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum f_i x_i^2}{\sum f_i} - \left(\frac{\sum f_i x_i}{\sum f_i}\right)^2}\)

🎲 Probability Basics

Classical probability
\(P(E)=\frac{\text{number of favourable outcomes}}{\text{total number of equally likely outcomes}}\)
Mutually exclusive events
\(P(A\cup B)=P(A)+P(B)\)
Complement rule
\(P(A')=1-P(A)\)
