🔢 Unit 7 – Digital Electronics 🥇 Easiest · 2–3 hours
📘 What to cover (only what appears in exams):
  • Logic gates: AND, OR, NOT, NAND, NOR, XOR – symbols & truth tables
  • De-Morgan’s theorems (both laws) – memorize & apply
  • Universal gates: NAND & NOR (why universal)
  • Flip-flop = 1‑bit storage element. Decade counter → 4 flip-flops
  • SOP / POS definition (basic)
  • XOR gate application: staircase switch logic (asked 2025)
⭐ No math · only tables & laws
⏱️ Time needed2–3 hours
📊 Expected marks8–12 marks
📅 Appears inEvery year (2021–25)
🔥 Priority#1 MUST DO
📈 Unit 6 – Operational Amplifiers 🥈 2 hours · high recall
📘 Core topics (very limited):
  • Ideal Op-Amp: Rin=∞, Aol=∞, Rout=0, CMRR=∞
  • Inverting amplifier gain: Vout = – (Rf/Ri)·Vin
  • Non-inverting gain: Vout = (1 + Rf/Ri)·Vin
  • Pin diagram of IC 741 (label all pins) – repeated every year
  • Virtual ground concept (one line)
  • Op-amp as adder (summing) – formula only
🎯 Two formulas + pinout = 80% questions
⏱️ Time needed2 hours
📊 Expected marks8–10 marks
📅 Appears in2021,22,23,24,25
🔥 Priority#2 (very safe)
🔌 Unit 1 – Electrical Components & Sources 🥉 1.5–2 hours
📘 Minimal theory + one numerical:
  • Ideal voltage source (internal resistance = 0) & ideal current source (internal resistance = ∞)
  • Source transformation: Vs = Is·R , Is = Vs/R
  • Active vs passive components (examples: diode, transistor vs resistor, capacitor)
  • Simple numerical on source transformation (find current / voltage)
⏱️ Time needed1.5–2 hours
📊 Expected marks4–8 marks
📅 Appears in2021,2022,2024
🔥 Priority#3 (quick win)
🔁 Unit 4 – Transformer (core only) ⚡ 2 hours · high weightage
📘 Focus ONLY on these (skip motor construction):
  • EMF equation: E = 4.44 f N Φm
  • Turns ratio: Vp/Vs = Np/Ns = Is/Ip
  • Transformer rating in kVA (why not kW)
  • Laminated core → reduces eddy current loss
  • Numerical: given Vp, Vs, Ns, find Np or flux or full load currents
⏱️ Time needed2 hours
📊 Expected marks6–10 marks
📅 Appears inEvery year (compulsory)
🔥 Priority#4 (non-negotiable)
🔄 Unit 3 – AC Circuits (selective) 📐 3–4 hours
📘 Only essential topics (skip complex exponentials):
  • RMS = Vm/√2 , Average = 2Vm/π, Peak factor=1.414, Form factor=1.11
  • XL=2πfL , XC=1/(2πfC) , Impedance Z=√(R²+(XL-XC)²)
  • Power factor cosφ = R/Z , Active power P=VI cosφ, Reactive power Q=VI sinφ
  • Must practice: Series RLC numerical – find I, pf, power, voltage drops
  • Phase relation: In L → current lags; in C → current leads (90°)
⏱️ Time needed3–4 hours
📊 Expected marks8–12 marks
📅 Appears inEvery year (1 long + MCQ)
🔥 Priority#5 (good but more formulas)

🧠 Quick Formula Card (for easiest units)

Op-Amp inv: Vout = –(Rf/Ri)·Vin
Non-inv: Vout = (1+Rf/Ri)·Vin
Transformer: Vp/Vs = Np/Ns , E = 4.44 f N Φm
Source transform: V = I·R , I = V/R
AC: Vrms = Vm/√2 , Z = √(R²+X²) , P = VI cosφ
De‑Morgan: (A+B)' = A'·B' , (AB)' = A'+B'
