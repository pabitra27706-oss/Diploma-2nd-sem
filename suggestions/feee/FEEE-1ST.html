🔢 Digital rules

De-Morgan: (A·B)' = A' + B' ; (A+B)' = A'·B'

AND: A·0=0, A·1=A, A·A=A

OR: A+1=1, A+0=A, A+A=A

NAND = AND followed by NOT, NOR = OR followed by NOT

Flip-flop stores 1 bit; decade counter = 4 FFs (2⁴=16 states, reset after 10)
