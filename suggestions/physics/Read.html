🥇 Unit 6 · Semiconductor Physics

Easiest ~3.5 hrs 8 Marks Long Q
  • Energy band diagrams – conductor vs semiconductor vs insulator
  • Intrinsic & extrinsic semiconductors; p‑type & n‑type doping
  • p‑n junction formation & depletion region
  • Forward & reverse bias – V‑I characteristics (draw graph)
  • Full‑wave rectifier (centre‑tapped) – circuit diagram & waveforms
  • Transistor (n‑p‑n) CE amplifier – working, α, β, relation β=α/(1‑α)
  • LED, solar cell – working principle (short notes)
✅ Guaranteed 8 marks – one full long question from this unit every year. Mostly descriptive with diagrams.

🥈 Unit 7 · Modern Physics

Very Easy ~3.5 hrs 8 Marks Long Q
  • X‑rays – Coolidge tube, continuous vs characteristic spectrum
  • Intensity vs wavelength graph (mark λmin, peaks)
  • Duane‑Hunt formula: λmin = 12400/V Å
  • LASER – principle, population inversion, properties (coherence, monochromatic)
  • He‑Ne laser energy level diagram
  • Optical fibre – propagation by total internal reflection, advantages
  • Bohr model – energy levels En = −13.6/n² eV, transition energy
✅ Guaranteed 8 marks – X‑rays + LASER + optical fibre appear almost every year in combination.

🥉 Unit 1 · Wave Motion & SHM

~3.5 hrs 8 Marks Long Q
  • SHM – definition, displacement, velocity, acceleration equations
  • vmax = Aω, amax = ω²A
  • Wave equation y = A sin(ωt − kx) – find f, λ, v from equation
  • Beats – definition, beat frequency = |f₁ − f₂|
  • Free, damped & forced vibrations (differences)
  • Ultrasonic waves – properties and medical/industrial uses
✅ Guaranteed 8 marks – SHM + wave equation are the core; beats/ultrasonic often fill 2‑mark sub‑questions.

🏅 Unit 2 · Optics

~3.5 hrs 8 Marks Long Q
  • Total internal reflection – critical angle, relation θc = sin⁻¹(1/μ)
  • Lens formula 1/f = 1/v − 1/u, magnification
  • Lens maker’s formula 1/f = (μ−1)(1/R₁ − 1/R₂)
  • Ray diagrams – convex lens (object between F and O → virtual image)
  • Simple & compound microscope (magnification formula)
  • Optical fibre – TIR based light propagation (overlap with Unit 7)
✅ Guaranteed 8 marks – TIR and lens numericals are favourite. Combine with optical fibre for easy marks.

📌 Unit 3 · Electrostatics

~3.5 hrs 8 Marks Long Q
  • Coulomb’s law (vector form) – force between two charges
  • Electric field due to a point charge
  • Gauss’s law statement & derivation for field outside a charged sphere
  • Capacitance – series & parallel combination formulas
  • Numerical: equivalent capacitance, energy stored U=½CV²
✅ Guaranteed 8 marks – Gauss law derivation is a fixed 5‑mark question; capacitors appear as 3‑mark numerical.
