The Story Behind This App

As a diploma student myself, I struggled to find organized study materials. Notes were scattered, previous papers were hard to find, and studying felt chaotic. I created this app to solve these problems for myself and my friends. Seeing it help other students has been incredibly rewarding.

"If it helped me, maybe it can help others too."
