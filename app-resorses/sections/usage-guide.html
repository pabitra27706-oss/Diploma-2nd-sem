How to Use This App

Get the most out of your study sessions

Complete User Guide

Learn how to navigate, study effectively, and use all features of 1st Semester Study Hub.

1

Get Started

When you open the app, you'll see the welcome screen. Tap "Get Started" to begin. The app will load your study materials (this happens once when you first open).

Tip: Make sure you have a stable internet connection the first time you open the app to download all study materials.
2

Choose Your Subject

You'll see a grid of subjects. Tap any subject to proceed. The progress bar at the top shows your navigation path.

📚

All subjects are available offline once loaded for the first time.

3

Select Content Type

After choosing a subject, select what you want to study:

📄

Study Materials

Notes, chapters, theory

📝

PYQs

Previous Year Questions

📊

Important Questions

Frequently asked questions

4

Browse & Study

You'll now see the available study materials organized by units or chapters. Tap any item to view it. Use the navigation buttons at the bottom to move between sections.

Navigation Tips:
  • Use the back button (←) to go back one step
  • Use the Home button to return to subjects
  • The "More" button takes you to this help section
5

Study Effectively

Here are some study strategies using this app:

🎯

Focus on Weak Areas

Use the app to identify and focus on subjects you find difficult

⏱️

Time Management

Study in short, focused sessions using the organized materials

🔄

Revision Strategy

Revise with PYQs to understand exam patterns
