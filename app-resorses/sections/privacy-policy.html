Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Our Commitment to Privacy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Important: We Don't Collect Personal Data

2nd Semester Study Hub does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information. The app works entirely offline after initial setup.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Personal Info

No names, emails, or contact details

Location Data

No GPS or location tracking

Device Access

No camera, microphone, or contacts access

Usage Analytics

No tracking of app usage or behavior

App Permissions Explained

The app requires minimal permissions for basic functionality:

Internet Access

Required only to download study materials initially. All data stays on your device.

Storage Access

Required to save study materials on your device for offline use. No personal files are accessed.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected, there is no data to secure. The study materials are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted anywhere.

No Data Collection Local Storage Only No Third-Party Sharing

Voluntary Contact

If you choose to contact us via email or Telegram:

  • Your email address is visible only to the developer
  • Messages are kept confidential and not shared
  • You can request deletion of your messages at any time
  • Contact information is used only to respond to your queries

Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to educational websites or resources. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for educational use by students of all ages. As no personal data is collected, there are no specific age restrictions. However, we encourage younger students to use the app under adult supervision.
