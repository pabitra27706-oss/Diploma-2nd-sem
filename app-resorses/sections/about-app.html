About This App

Purpose, Features & Vision

What is 1st Semester Study Hub?

1st Semester Study Hub is an educational Android application designed specifically for diploma students to prepare effectively for their first semester examinations. It brings together previous year question papers (PYQs), organized study materials, and subject-wise navigation in one simple, offline-friendly platform.

Built with a clean interface, students can easily navigate between subjects, units, and chapters without confusion. The app focuses on making exam preparation structured, accessible, and stress-free.

Our Mission & Purpose

This app was created with one simple goal: to help students succeed.

  • Make quality study materials accessible to every student
  • Reduce exam anxiety through organized preparation
  • Save students' time in searching for scattered resources
  • Provide offline access for studying anywhere, anytime
  • Help understand exam patterns through previous papers
  • Create a community-driven learning platform
